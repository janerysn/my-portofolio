Information System Design for "Sultan Barber.app"

The design is carried out to fulfill the final project for the Object-Oriented Design course.
The design is conducted in a group and is based on real case studies and problems.
The creation aims to facilitate users (Manager, Consumers, Employees) in the daily operational
continuity through the available features. The final form of the design process is a prototype
Mockup and Wireframe by Figma
